--- title: Surveillance of Soil-transmitted Helminths and Schistosomiasis description: Geostatistical modelling and survey design for soil-transmitted helminths and schistosomiasis in India, Nigeria, Kenya and Pakistan in collaboration with Evidence Action expiry: 2025-10-31 people: - clark - giorgi links: - url: https://www.evidenceaction.org/programs/deworm-the-world description: Funder ---

Soil-transmitted helminths (STH) and schistosomiasis are neglected tropical diseases caused by infection of parasitic worms, primarily in low-middle income countries. As part of their Deworm the World programme, we are collaborating with Evidence Action to provide governments in India, Nigeria, Kenya and Pakistan with data-driven evidence to inform policy for treatment and surveillance. We use geostatistics to evaluate the impact of mass drug administration and compare survey designs to find the most cost-effective approach to surveillance.
