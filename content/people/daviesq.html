I am a PhD student at Lancaster University, supervised by Jo Knight and Nicola Rennie. My research focuses on federated analytics within the NHS, specifically looking at methods for standardising and analysing health data. In addition to my studies, I work full-time as a Data Scientist at Lancashire Teaching Hospitals.

I hold a BSc (Hons) in Mathematics and Statistics from the Open University, and an MSc in Health Data Science from Lancaster University.
