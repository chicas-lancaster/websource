Jana is a PhD student in Statistics and Epidemiology supervised by Emanuele Giorgi. She joined CHICAS in 2022 as an MSc Health Data Science student and went on to complete a year as a research associate as part of an NIHR fellowship. Her research is in epidemiology and the application of geospatial statistics and analysis. Specifically, she works on applying geostatistical modelling to neglected tropical diseases.
