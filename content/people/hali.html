I am Heba Ali, a Senior Research Associate in the Health Economics Department at DHR. I have a background in health and healthcare research. Trained as a physician, I practiced for a few years before transitioning into research. I hold a master’s degree in Health Economics and a PhD in Health Policy. My research interests focus on healthcare delivery, including studies on team coordination, quality of care, provider well-being, and the impact of public policies on patient outcomes. I’m currently working on an evaluation study of a AI-enabled MedTech device installed in care homes to detect resident falls and rapidly alert staff to intervene.
